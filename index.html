Fitness during COVID period

The COVID-19 pandemic means that many of us are staying at home and sitting down more than we usually do. It’s hard for a lot of us to do the sort of exercise we normally do. It’s even harder for people who don’t usually do a lot of physical exercise. Regular physical activity can help give our days a routine and be a way to stay in contact with family and friends

About

FITNESS CLUB offers you a professional and innovative outdoor training program with which you can improve your performance in sport and your fitness in everyday life. Our program is based on training methods from competitive sports and covers the most important athletic performance parameters in a 6-month cycle:

Power

Speed

Agility

Endurance

With our concept, we want to create a platform for athletes, amateur athletes and everyday heroes to be able to participate in structured and progressive athletic training without having to be tied to a gym or spend the money on a personal trainer.

Be

In almost any wind and weather in small groups of a maximum of 15 people. It is trained twice a week for 60 minutes each. The training units are intense and designed to improve your athletic performance.
